WANT TO START A SOLAR PROJECT IN YOUR COMMUNITY?

Is there a nonprofit organization or a cooperative in your community that you really care about? Would you like to see that organization save money on its electric bills and be a climate leader by going solar? If so, you can help make this happen!
 
By filling out this application you are expressing interest in being the point person to lead a solar energy campaign for a community serving organization you care about. To get started, we’ll need some information about you and the organization you want to put solar on.

Background


As you know, RE-volv finances solar energy projects for nonprofits and cooperatives that serve their community. RE-volv allows people like you to spearhead crowdfunding campaigns to cover the initial costs of a solar installation. (Translation: running an online fundraiser and collecting tax deductible donations.) As the nonprofit or co-op pays RE-volv back through a solar lease agreement over time, people who gave to the campaign will get to choose what solar projects to reinvest those funds into next! All of these projects contribute to the Solar Seed Fund, our revolving fund for solar energy!

Benefits to Organization

  • Go solar for no up-front costs
  • Save up to 15% or more on electric bills
  • Power your facility with clean energy and reduce carbon emissions
  • Be recognized as a leader in your community
  • Give your friends the ability to support your organization’s commitment to solar energy
